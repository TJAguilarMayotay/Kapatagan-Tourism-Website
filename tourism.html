Discover Kapatagan's Treasures

Explore the breathtaking tourist spots that showcase the natural beauty and cultural heritage of our municipality.

Cathedral Falls

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Cathedral Falls in Kapatagan, Lanao Del Norte, where majestic waters cascade against stunning rock formations, creating a truly awe-inspiring natural wonder.

Durano View

Experience the stunning, elevated perspective of Durano View & Mountain Resort, offering a tranquil escape with picturesque landscapes.

La Liberad Lake

Discover the tranquil beauty of La Libertad Lake in Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte, where a serene man-made lake surrounded by lush greenery offers a perfect escape for boating, kayaking, fishing, and peaceful relaxation amidst nature's embrace.

Santa Cruz Falls

Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Santa Cruz Falls in Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte, where a magnificent multi-tiered waterfall cascades into inviting natural pools amidst a lush, untouched forest, offering a truly refreshing and adventurous escape.

Waterworld

Dive into fun at Waterworld in Kapatagan, Lanao del Norte, a vibrant aquatic park offering thrilling slides, spacious pools, and exciting water attractions perfect for a memorable family day out.
